Place your SDXL checkpoint files (.safetensors) in this directory.

Supported models:
- Base SDXL checkpoints
- SDXL derivatives (NoobAI, Illustrious, Pony, etc.)
- Custom trained SDXL models

File format: .safetensors
Minimum required: 1 model (for single model operations)
Recommended: 2-3 models for optimal merging results
