Place your LoRA files (.safetensors) in this directory.

Notes:
- LoRAs are only supported in Legacy mode
- PerRes mode does not support LoRA baking
- Supported format: .safetensors
- Compatible with SDXL LoRAs only

Usage:
- Select LoRAs during Legacy mode merge process
- Specify scale/strength for each LoRA (recommended: 0.3-0.8)
- LoRAs will be permanently baked into the merged model
